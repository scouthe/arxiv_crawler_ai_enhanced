【1】A 4R-supported circular product-service system for luxury branded events

标题 : 4R支持的奢侈品牌活动循环产品服务体系
链接 : https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20613
作者：Ke Ma,Francesca Valsecchi,Yuchen Tan,Mingjia Ji,Junru Shen,Xiaoya Ma,Duan Wu,Jiao Mo,Shijian Zhao
摘要：临时奢侈品牌活动的周期短，定制的活动会加速材料的流失。我们提出了一个循环物理产品服务系统，通过跨仓库到活动旅程的 4R 框架（垃圾、减少、再利用和回收）来运营循环经济 (CE)。该系统是通过与一级承包商进行多方法设计调查而开发的，将物理接触点（可重复使用的折叠平装运输箱、可调节货架、标准标签）与数字编排（实时数字仓库、基于列表的出库/入库工作流程和可持续材料库）结合起来。该架构协调角色和决策，保护和识别资产，并使重用成为奢侈品牌约束下的默认设置。通过将可追溯的操作和与 CE 一致的规则嵌入到日常交接中，PSS 将采购、存储、调度、退货和重新部署转向保值。这一贡献是一条可复制、可实践的路线，从循环意图到品牌环境中的运营变革，在不影响速度或审美标准的情况下推进负责任的零售。
Abstract：Temporary luxury branded events run on short cycles and bespoke builds that accelerate material churn. We present a circular phygital product-service system that operationalises the circular economy (CE) through a 4R frame (Refuse, Reduce, Reuse, and Recycling) across warehouse-to-event journeys. Developed via a multi-method design inquiry with a tier-1 contractor, the system couples physical touchpoints (reusable fold-flat transit boxes, adjustable racking, standard labels) with digital orchestration (a live digital warehouse, list-based outbound/inbound workflow, and a sustainable materials library). The architecture aligns roles and decisions, protects and identifies assets, and makes reuse the default under luxury brand constraints. By embedding traceable actions and CE-aligned rules into everyday handoffs, the PSS shifts procurement, storage, dispatch, return, and redeployment toward value retention. The contribution is a replicable, practice-ready route from circular intent to operational change in branded environments, advancing responsible retail without compromising speed or aesthetic standards.

Moreover, we explore the virtual reconstruction between mesh reconstruction and 3D gaussian splatting to serve as different modalities for documentation. 摘要：再入院仍然是医疗保健系统面临的一个挑战，尤其是患有糖尿病等慢性病的患者。 30 天内计划外的再入院成本高昂，医院资源紧张，并且可能表明护理协调或出院计划不善。在这项工作中，我们探索使用机器学习来预测糖尿病住院患者的再入院风险，并提出混合现实（MR）来提供有效的可视化和见解。经过数据清理、编码和特征工程后，我们训练了 XGBoost 分类器。该模型的接收器工作特性曲线下面积 (AUROC) 为 0.72，精确回忆曲线下面积 (AUPRC) 为 0.11。关键预测因素包括既往住院就诊、出院处置和血糖控制指标，例如 A1C（血糖测试）结果和药物调整。此外，我们开发了一个 MR 原型，可以可视化患者记录和预测，其中包含风险水平、主要影响因素和简明的护理摘要。预测模型和 MR 界面旨在提高临床医生对实时临床环境中再入院风险的认识和沟通。
Abstract：Hospital readmissions remain a challenge for healthcare systems, especially among patients with chronic conditions such as diabetes. Key predictive factors included prior inpatient visits, discharge disposition, and glycemic control indicators such as A1C (blood sugar test) results and medication adjustments. Additionally, we developed an MR prototype that visualize patient records and predictions containing risk level, major contributing factors, and a concise summary of care. Together, the predictive model and the MR interface aim to improve clinician awareness and communication around readmission risk in real-time clinical settings.

Abstract：Sexual and reproductive health (SRH) remains shaped by structural barriers that leave many without judgment-free information. Evaluating SRH-specific non-LLM chatbots, general-use LLMs, and search engine features along credibility and reasoning, we identify two epistemic harms: source opacity and response rigidity. We conclude with design and evaluation recommendations, participatory auditing strategies, and policy implications for high-stakes domains where AI intersects with inequity.

【1】CounterScene: Counterfactual Causal Reasoning in Generative World Models for Safety-Critical Closed-Loop Evaluation

标题 : CounterScene：用于安全关键闭环评估的生成世界模型中的反事实因果推理
链接 : https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21104
作者：Bowen Jing,Ruiyang Hao,Weitao Zhou,Haibao Yu
摘要：生成安全关键的驾驶场景需要了解为什么会出现危险的相互作用，而不仅仅是强行碰撞。然而，现有方法依赖于启发式对抗性代理选择和非结构化扰动，缺乏交互依赖性的显式建模，从而表现出现实性——对抗性权衡。我们提出了 CounterScene，这是一个框架，为闭环生成 BEV 世界模型赋予结构化反事实推理，以生成安全关键场景。 CounterScene 提出一个安全场景：如果因果关系关键的智能体表现不同怎么办？为了回答这个问题，我们引入因果对抗代理识别来识别关键代理并对冲突类型进行分类，并开发一个冲突感知的交互式世界模型，其中因果交互图用于显式地建模动态代理间依赖关系。在此结构的基础上，阶段自适应反事实指导对已识别的代理执行最少的干预，消除其空间和时间安全裕度，同时允许通过自然交互传播出现风险。 nuScenes 上的大量实验表明，CounterScene 实现了最强的对抗有效性，同时在所有视野中保持卓越的轨迹真实感，将长视野碰撞率从最强基线的 12.3% 提高到 22.7%，并具有更好的真实感（ADE 1.88 vs.2.09）。值得注意的是，这种优势随着时间的推移而进一步扩大，CounterScene 将零样本推广到具有最先进现实主义的 nuPlan。
Abstract：Generating safety-critical driving scenarios requires understanding why dangerous interactions arise, rather than merely forcing collisions. However, existing methods rely on heuristic adversarial agent selection and unstructured perturbations, lacking explicit modeling of interaction dependencies and thus exhibiting a realism--adversarial trade-off. We present CounterScene, a framework that endows closed-loop generative BEV world models with structured counterfactual reasoning for safety-critical scenario generation. Given a safe scene, CounterScene asks: what if the causally critical agent had behaved differently? To answer this, we introduce causal adversarial agent identification to identify the critical agent and classify conflict types, and develop a conflict-aware interactive world model in which a causal interaction graph is used to explicitly model dynamic inter-agent dependencies. Building on this structure, stage-adaptive counterfactual guidance performs minimal interventions on the identified agent, removing its spatial and temporal safety margins while allowing risk to emerge through natural interaction propagation. Extensive experiments on nuScenes demonstrate that CounterScene achieves the strongest adversarial effectiveness while maintaining superior trajectory realism across all horizons, improving long-horizon collision rate from 12.3% to 22.7% over the strongest baseline with better realism (ADE 1.88 vs.2.09). Notably, this advantage further widens over longer rollouts, and CounterScene generalizes zero-shot to nuPlan with state-of-the-art realism.

 


【2】Cortical Policy: A Dual-Stream View Transformer for Robotic Manipulation

标题 : 皮质策略：用于机器人操作的双流视图转换器
链接 : https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21051
作者：Xuening Zhang,Qi Lv,Xiang Deng,Miao Zhang,Xingbo Liu,Liqiang Nie
摘要：视图转换器处理多视图观察来预测动作，并在机器人操作中表现出令人印象深刻的性能。现有方法通常以特定于视图的方式提取静态视觉表示，导致 3D 空间推理能力不足且缺乏动态适应。从人类大脑如何整合静态和动态视图来应对这些挑战中汲取灵感，我们提出了 Cortical Policy，这是一种用于机器人操作的新型双流视图转换器，可以从静态视图和动态视图流中联合推理。静态视图流通过对齐从预训练的 3D 基础模型中提取的几何一致关键点的特征来增强空间理解。动态视图流通过自我中心注视估计模型的位置感知预训练实现自适应调整，通过计算复制人类皮质背侧通路。随后，集成两个流的互补视图表示以确定最终动作，使模型能够在语言条件下处理空间复杂且动态变化的任务。对 RLBench、具有挑战性的 COLOSSEUM 基准和现实世界任务的实证评估表明，皮质策略的性能大大优于最先进的基线，验证了双流设计在视觉运动控制方面的优越性。我们受皮质启发的框架为机器人操作提供了全新的视角，并在基于视觉的机器人控制中具有更广泛应用的潜力。
Abstract：View transformers process multi-view observations to predict actions and have shown impressive performance in robotic manipulation. Existing methods typically extract static visual representations in a view-specific manner, leading to inadequate 3D spatial reasoning ability and a lack of dynamic adaptation. Taking inspiration from how the human brain integrates static and dynamic views to address these challenges, we propose Cortical Policy, a novel dual-stream view transformer for robotic manipulation that jointly reasons from static-view and dynamic-view streams. The static-view stream enhances spatial understanding by aligning features of geometrically consistent keypoints extracted from a pretrained 3D foundation model. The dynamic-view stream achieves adaptive adjustment through position-aware pretraining of an egocentric gaze estimation model, computationally replicating the human cortical dorsal pathway. Subsequently, the complementary view representations of both streams are integrated to determine the final actions, enabling the model to handle spatially-complex and dynamically-changing tasks under language conditions. Empirical evaluations on RLBench, the challenging COLOSSEUM benchmark, and real-world tasks demonstrate that Cortical Policy outperforms state-of-the-art baselines substantially, validating the superiority of dual-stream design for visuomotor control. Our cortex-inspired framework offers a fresh perspective for robotic manipulation and holds potential for broader application in vision-based robot control.

 


【3】Dreaming the Unseen: World Model-regularized Diffusion Policy for Out-of-Distribution Robustness

标题 : 梦想看不见的事物：世界模型规范化的扩散策略以实现分布外的鲁棒性
链接 : https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21017

摘要：扩散策略在视觉运动控制方面表现出色，但在严重的分布外（OOD）干扰（例如意外的物体位移或视觉损坏）下常常会发生灾难性的失败。为了解决这个漏洞，我们引入了梦想扩散策略（DDP），该框架通过共享的 3D 视觉编码器将扩散世界模型深度集成到策略的训练目标中。这种协同优化赋予策略强大的状态预测能力。当推理过程中遇到突发的OOD异常时，DDP会检测到真实想象的差异并主动放弃损坏的视觉流。相反，它依靠其内部的“想象力”（自回归预测的潜在动态）来安全地绕过干扰，在顺利地与物理现实重新对齐之前生成想象的轨迹。广泛的评估证明了 DDP 卓越的弹性。值得注意的是，DDP 在 MetaWorld 上实现了 73.8% 的 OOD 成功率（没有预测想象的情况下为 23.9%），在严重的现实世界空间变化下的成功率为 83.3%（没有预测想象的情况下为 3.3%）。此外，作为压力测试，即使完全依赖初始化后的开环想象，DDP 也能保持 76.7% 的现实成功率。
Abstract：Diffusion policies excel at visuomotor control but often fail catastrophically under severe out-of-distribution (OOD) disturbances, such as unexpected object displacements or visual corruptions. To address this vulnerability, we introduce the Dream Diffusion Policy (DDP), a framework that deeply integrates a diffusion world model into the policy's training objective via a shared 3D visual encoder. This co-optimization endows the policy with robust state-prediction capabilities. When encountering sudden OOD anomalies during inference, DDP detects the real-imagination discrepancy and actively abandons the corrupted visual stream. Instead, it relies on its internal "imagination" (autoregressively forecasted latent dynamics) to safely bypass the disruption, generating imagined trajectories before smoothly realigning with physical reality. Extensive evaluations demonstrate DDP's exceptional resilience. Notably, DDP achieves a 73.8% OOD success rate on MetaWorld (vs. 23.9% without predictive imagination) and an 83.3% success rate under severe real-world spatial shifts (vs. 3.3% without predictive imagination). Furthermore, as a stress test, DDP maintains a 76.7% real-world success rate even when relying entirely on open-loop imagination post-initialization.

 


【4】Geometrically Plausible Object Pose Refinement using Differentiable Simulation

Abstract：State-of-the-art object pose estimation methods are prone to generating geometrically infeasible pose hypotheses. Furthermore, the improvement in geometric plausibility is accompanied by a concurrent reduction in translation and orientation errors. Achieving pose estimation that is grounded in physical reality while remaining faithful to multi-modal sensor inputs is a critical step toward robust in-hand manipulation.

标题 : 通过可验证因子图优化实现鲁棒 M 估计器
